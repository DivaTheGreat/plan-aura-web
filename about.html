About PlanAura

Built for Women Who Refuse to Drift

Diva | PlanAura exists for the Catholic woman who knows there is more — and is ready to live it with intention.

Our Story

Why PlanAura Exists

Diva | PlanAura was born from a simple but urgent observation: too many faith-driven women are living reactive lives. They are overcommitted, under-resourced, financially stressed, and spiritually scattered — not because they lack desire, but because they lack structure.

We believe that faith, finances, and productivity are not separate domains. They are three expressions of the same thing: how you steward your life. When one is disordered, all three suffer. When all three are aligned, you step into a version of yourself that is calm, intentional, and deeply effective.

PlanAura exists to give you the tools, frameworks, and community to build that kind of life — rooted in your Catholic faith, grounded in practical wisdom, and designed for real, ordinary days.

Our Three Pillars

The PlanAura Framework

Every resource we create lives under one of these three pillars — and together, they form a complete framework for an intentional life.

✝
Faith · Catholic

Tools, calendars, and guides for deepening your prayer life, living the liturgical year, and building habits rooted in Catholic tradition. Your faith is the foundation — not a category.

◈
Personal Finance

Worksheets, frameworks, and guides for budgeting with intention, building savings, and approaching money as a form of spiritual stewardship — not just a practical task.

◆
Productivity

Planning tools, routine guides, and frameworks to help you structure your week, protect your focus, and build systems that create space for the people and things that matter most.
